The upper left window shows a hierarchical tree of commits and their 
corresponding relationships with branches. It also gives information about 
branches. Below are the SHA 1 value of the selected commit.

The middle window with the name and emails shows the corresponding authors' 
information about commits in the same row in the left window.

The right windwo shows the corresponding date of commits in the same row.

The lower left window shows the changelog of the comits, usually with the 
actual file changes in diff format.

The lower right windwo displays the files impacted by the selected commit.
